The Story

An epic journey across the Great Universal Realm

When the Darkness Came

In the peaceful Kingdom of Nuvolandia, the magical parchment that maintains harmony among the seven kingdoms of the Great Universal Kingdom, known everywhere as Sigillum Maximum, is destroyed by the mysterious Black Knight. The kingdom and its inhabitants are turned to stone.

The only one to escape the spell is a thirteen-year-old girl: Princess Aileen. Thanks to the elves, she discovers that the only way to save her world is to recover the seven magical secrets that, when united, can bring the Sigillum back to life.

"Seven secrets. Seven magical months. Seven real years. The end of the world — or its salvation."

Inexperienced and frightened, but driven by the desire to free her people, Aileen sets out on an extraordinary journey accompanied by the young warrior Dorcha and the valiant orc-troll Grogher. As time runs inexorably and war between the peoples approaches, their mission becomes the discovery of who they truly are.

But Aileen will make a devastating discovery that will shake everything she believes about love, loyalty, and the nature of good and evil. The greatest power is not magic — it is the ability to change.

Some secrets are worth dying for. Some choices define who we become. And some love is strong enough to rewrite destiny itself.

The Seven Realms

A universe built from light, shadow, and ancient magic

☁️

Nuvolandia

The Cloud Kingdom. A land of soft clouds, moonstone castles, and beings in constant telepathic contact with the skies. Home of Princess Aileen and her father King Eadwig.

🌿

Realm of the Green Forests

An ancient realm of towering forests and luminous cities. Home to the elves, King Baelkers, and Aeltiàfisar, Knight of the Golden Light.

⛰️

Realm of the Two Rainbows

The Gnome Kingdom. A steep cliff divided into fourteen villages: seven colors of the sun rainbow, seven of the moon rainbow. Home to Pallafiocco tournaments and ancient curses.

🌊

Dark Abyssal Seas

The Merfolk Kingdom. An underwater realm of coral palaces in the depths of the sea. The merpeople hold secrets that even the oldest elves do not know.

🏔️

Sliabh Mountain Chains

An enormous mountain range inhabited by Trolls and Mountain Gnomes. Forged in the heart of mountains, masters of craft, stone, and ancient metallurgy.

🧚

Realm of the Magic Wings

Divided between the inner realm inhabited by fairies and the outer realm inhabited by goblins. A place of wonder and mischief, now devastated by dark forces.

⚡

Realm of the Storm

A realm of perpetual tempests. Home to the orcs and their queen Urchoicha. A people of immense strength, deep loyalty, and a secret buried in thunder.

The Author

The voice behind the magic

EL

Eleanor Lian

Author & Creator

Eleanor Lian is a storyteller who builds worlds where emotions have weight, magic has rules, and every character carries a piece of the human heart. Sigillum Maximum — The Seal of the Seven Secrets is her most ambitious work: an epic fantasy that explores courage, loss, love, and the choices that define who we become.

"The power most great is not magic. It is the ability to change."

Themes: identity and self-discovery, conflict between fate and free will, the transformative power of love, sacrifice, balance between good and evil, responsibility toward the world. At the center is a question that crosses every realm: are we truly free to choose who we become, or are we prisoners of the destiny others wrote for us?
